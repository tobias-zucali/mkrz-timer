A timer focused on simple sharing, live sessions, accessibility, and distraction-free usage.

mkrz timer was originally created for workshops, facilitation sessions, classrooms, collaborative projects, and group processes where reliable timekeeping matters.

Over time, it has also proven useful for sports, exercise routines, training sessions, presentations, study sessions, meetings, and personal productivity.

## What is mkrz timer?

At its core, mkrz timer is simply a timer.

The goal is to provide a timer that is easy to use, easy to share, and easy to understand.

Whether you are working alone, presenting to a group, leading a workshop, running an exercise session, or coordinating participants across multiple devices, mkrz timer is designed to stay focused on timing rather than getting in the way.

The project is a personal side project by Tobias Zucali, Co-Founder of [mkrz lab](https://www.mkrz.at/).

## How live session sharing works

### Local timer

Use the timer on a single device.

- no account required
- timer data stays in your browser
- no live session is created
- ideal for personal use

### Live session

Create a session and keep multiple devices in sync.

1. Create a live session.
2. Share the join link with participants or other devices.
3. Keep the manage link for yourself.
4. Timer changes stay synchronized across connected devices.

This works well for workshops, presentations, classrooms, exercise sessions, hybrid participation, and distributed groups.

## Privacy by design

mkrz timer is designed to be usable without creating an account.

For local timers:

- no account is required
- timer data remains on your device
- no live session is created

When using live sessions, timer state is shared only with people who have the corresponding join or manage link.

Learn more on the [Privacy](/privacy) page.
